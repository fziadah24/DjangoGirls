ITS POSTER SESSION DAY!!!! THIS MORNING WE GOT UP AT 5 FOR THE FIRST TIME! MAYAH AND KIERSTEN WANTED TO SLEEP LATER SO WE PACKED UP AND WENT TO BLU'S MOMS ROOM SO WE DIDN'T WAKE THEM. WE BROUGHT OUR STUFF DOWN TO THE LOBBY AND PUT IT IN THE BACK ROOM FOR THEMT O HOLD, THEN WENT TO GET BREAKFAST. JEFF CAME AND SAT WITH US. THEN WE HEADED TO THE CONVENTION! BLU'S HIP WERE DYING SO THEY GOT A WHEELCHAIR FOR THE DAY. IT WAS BRIGHT YELLOW! WE THEN HEADED TO CHECK OUT OUR POSTER!

OUR POSTER WAS STILL UP SO WE GOT BREAKFAST AND SAT AT A TABLE. ERIC SNOW, A PYTHON CORE DEVELOPER CAME TO SIT WITH US. HE TALKED TO US ABOUT HOW HE GOT TO WHERE HE IS AND HOW BE BUILT HIS COMMUNITY AT PYCON. HE WAS SUPER INTERESTED IN OUR NASA PROJECT AND WAS IMPRESSED AT OUR ABILITIES SO YOUNG. WE WALKED OVER TO THE LIGHTNING TALKS AND SAT IN THE BACK. WE FOUND THAT THEY WEREN'T AS INTERESTING TODAY, SO WE DECIDED TO LEAVE AND PREPARE FOR OUR POSTER SESSION!

OUR POSTER SESSION STARTED AND WE HAD SO MANY PEOPLE INTERESTED IN WH0 WE ARE AND WHAT WE ARE DOING. SOME NASA SOFWARE DEELOPERS CAME BY AND GAVE US PINS. A LEADER FROM PYOHIO CAME AND ASKED US TO SUBMIT A TALK PROPOSAL. ERIC SNOW, WHO TALKED TO US EARLIER IN THE DAY CAME BY TO CHECK OUT OUR POSTER! EVERYONE WAS HUNGRY SO WE LEFT OUR POSTER TO GET LUNCH. IT WAS GREAT!

AFTER THE POSTER SESSION, WE SAT OUTSIDE THE EXPO HALL AND DID HOMEWORK. WE GOT A LOT DONE!!! AS WE SAT THERE, THE WORKERS STARTED TAKING THINGS DOWN AND THE PLACE CLEARED OUT. WE SAW SO MANY BIKERS AND BOATS ON THE WATER, SO WE DECIDED TO GO OUTSIDE AND GET SOME FRESH AIR.

AFTER THE KEYNOTE IT WAS TIME TO HEAD HOME. JEFF PICKED UP THE VAN WHILE WE GRABBED OUR STUFF FROM THE HOTEL. WE ALL PREPARED FOR OUR LONG DRIVE HOME. ON OUR WAY HOME WE DECIDED WHERE WE WANTED TO GO FOR DINNER. WE STOPPED AT AN OLIVE GARDEN, BUT ENDED UP GOING TO A BURGER KING INSTEAD. WE FINALLY GOT HOME AROUND 9 PM. GOODNIGHT!!
