WE SET AN ALARM FOR 5:00 BUT WE ACTULLY GOT UP AT 5:45. BLU WENT TO THE GYM WITH HER MOM, THEN AFTERWARDS, OLIVE AND BLU WENT DOWNSTAIRS TO BREAKFAST. AS SOON AS WE GOT DOWN TO THE LOBBY, WE NOTICED JEFF AT ON OF THE TABLES GRADING EMAILS. HE WAS SO FOCUSED HE DIDN'T EVEN SEE US. WE WALKED OVER TO SAY HI, THEN WE GOT TO MEET ONE OF JEFF'S FRIENDS THAT COMES TO PYCON EVERY YEAR. HE GAVE US REALLY NICE MOLESKIN NOTEBOOKS!! WE ATE BREAKFAST THEN WENT BACK TO THE ROOM TO GET READY FOR THE DAY.

WE DECIDED THAT WE WANTED STARBUCKS SO WE STOPPED BY ON THE WAY TO THE CONVENTION CENTER. IT WAS DELICIOUS! WE GOT TO THE CONVENTION ADN PICKED UP T-SHIRTS AND SWAG AT THE SWAG TABLE. WE GOT THERE EARLY ENOUGH THAT THE EXPO HALL A WASN'T OPEN YET, BUT WE DECIDED TO WALK AROUND ANYWAY. WE GOT SOME STICKERS, SOME OTHER SMALL GIVEAWAYS THEN GOT BREAKFAST. ONCE THE EXPO HALL OPENED, WE SAT IN ON SOME DEMOS. OUR FAVORITE WAS ONE ON ANVIL AND PY.SPACE. AS STUDENTS WHO ARE ACTIVELY LEARNING PYTHON, IT COULD BE A GREAT TOOL TO USE. BLU ENDED UP DOWNLOADING ANVIL AND MAKING AN APP ON IT! WE THEN HEADED TO EXPO HALL B FOR THE OPENING KEYNOTE. JEFF WANTED US TO GET GOOD SEATS, SO WE WERE ABOUT 15 MINUTES EARLY. SLOWLY MORE PEOPLE STARTED SHOWING UP AS WELL AS A FEW MORE OF OUR GROUP MEMBERS.

WE FINALLY MADE IT TO THE EDUCATION SUMMIT! JEFF WAS STILL OUT PARKING THE VAN SO WE SAT IN LISTENING TO THE FIRST FEW TALKS. OLIVE AND BLU TOOK SOME NOTES ON THINGS WE FOUND INTERESTING. EVENTUALLY IT WAS TIME TO TAKE A BREAK SO OLIVE, BLU AND SHEEL WALKED BACK TO THE HOTEL SO WE COULD GRAB SOME STUFF WE WANTED FOR THE DAY. WE THEN HEADED BACK TO THE CONVENTION CENTER!

AFTER GETTING BACK TO THE CONVENTION CENTER, WE DECIDED TO GO TO EXPO HALL A AND SEE IF WE COULD HANG OUR POSTER EARLY. UNFORTUNATLY, IT WASNT SET UP YET. SO WE WAITED. AND WAITED. AND WAITED SOME MORE. FINALLY, THEY GOT IT SET UP AND WE WERE ABLE TO PUT UP OUR POSTER. IT LOOKED GREAT!

WE MET GUIDO VAN ROSSUM!!!! THE CREATOR OF PYTHON!!! HE SAID THAT HE USUALLY DOESN'T TAKE PHOTOS WITH PEOPLE BECAUSE THERE IS A CARDBOARD CUTOUT OF HIM, BUT FOR US, HE WILL.

AFTER MEETING GUIDO, WE DECIDED TO HEAD DOWN TO LUNCH! IT WAS ON THE MAIN FLOOR IN A BALLROOM WITH LOTS OF SHINY CEILING LIGHTS. LUNCH WAS PERFECT SO WE MADE OUR WAY BACK TO THE EDUCATION SUMMIT. JEFF GAVE A LIGHTNING TALK ABOUT DE COURSES AT ARLINGTON TECH!!! HE DID SO GOOD. WE GOT TO SHOUT, "ITS ALL JUST BITS!" DR. CHUCK ALSO GAVE A LIGHTNING TALK! WE THEN GOT INTO GROUPS AND DISCUSSED DR. CHUCK'S DJANGO COURSE.

OUR ROOMS WERE READY AT 3PM, SO WE HEADED BACK TO THE HOTEL TO CHECK IN. WE GOT TO OUR ROOMS AND ALL DECIDED THAT WE WERE TOO EXHAUSTED TO ATTEND THE OPENING RECEPTION. INSTEAD, WE GRABBED FREE POPCORN FROM THE LOBBY, AND COMPLETED HOMEWORK FOR CLASSES WE WERE MISSING. EVENTUALLY WE ENDED UP DOWN AT THE POOL. OLIVE DECIDED SHE HAD TOO MUCH WORK TO DO AND LOCKED-IN AT THE POOL.

DINNER! AFTER AN EXHAUSTING DAY, WE DECIDED TO JUST HAVE DINNER AT THE HOTEL. DINNER WAS MAC AND CHEESE, HOT DOGS AND NACHOS. NO VEGAN FOOD WOMP WOMP. BLU NEEDED ART SUPPLIES AND OLIVE NEEDED FOOD SO WE WALKED 7 MIN TO TARGET. WE ENDED UP GETTING FROZEN FOOD AND A HUGE SHARPIE. MAYAH AND KIERSTEN DISAPPEAR SO WE WAITED OUTSIDE FOR THEM TO COME BACK. WHILE WAITING OUTSIDE WE DECIDED THAT PITTSBURGH WASN'T A VERY SECURE CITY. THEN, WE WALKED BACK TO THE HOTEL.

WE GOT BACK TO THE ROOM AND MADE SOME FOOD, THEN OLIVE AND BLU WENT UP TO THE TERRACE TO DO HOMEWORK. BECAUSE WE ARE ALL HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS, WE HAD LOTS OF WORK FROM THE DAYS OF SCHOOL THAT WE WERE MISSING FOR THE CONFERENCE. WE WERE ALL SO TIRED SO WE WATCHED THE SUN SET THEN GOT READY TO SLEEP.
